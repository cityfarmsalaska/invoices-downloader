Privacy Policy

QBO Invoice Downloader is an internal tool used to connect to QuickBooks Online (QBO) and download invoice PDFs and limited invoice metadata for the signed-in company.

Data We Access

  • Invoice data (IDs, numbers, dates, totals) and invoice PDFs.
  • OAuth tokens provided by Intuit for API access.

How We Use Data

  • Solely to download invoice PDFs to the user-selected folder on the local computer.
  • No selling, sharing, or advertising use.

Storage and Security

  • Access and refresh tokens are stored locally in Windows Credential Manager via the OS keychain.
  • Downloaded PDFs are stored only in the folder you choose.

Retention

  • Tokens are retained until you revoke access or remove them.
  • You may delete PDFs at any time from your filesystem.

Your Choices

  • You can disconnect the app in QuickBooks Online at any time.
  • You can clear stored tokens using the tool’s command: manage_qbo_secrets.py clear realm_id and remove token entries.
